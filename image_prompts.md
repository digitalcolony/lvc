# Image Generation Prompts for Lee Van Cleef Tribute



## Illustrated Image Generation Prompt Template

**Base Prompt:**
```
Illustrated portrait of Lee Van Cleef as [CHARACTER NAME] in [MOVIE TITLE], vintage Western movie poster art style, hand-drawn illustration, ink and watercolor technique, 1960s-1970s Spaghetti Western poster aesthetic, painterly brushstrokes, artistic interpretation
```

## Style Specifications

- **Art Style:** Vintage movie poster illustration, reminiscent of 1960s/70s Western film posters
- **Medium:** Ink line art with watercolor washes, or gouache painting style
- **Technique:** Hand-drawn appearance with visible brushstrokes, crosshatching for shadows
- **Color Palette:** 
  - Warm sepia and ochre tones
  - Gunmetal gray (#2C3539) for shadows
  - Leather brown (#8B4513) and dusty gold (#C2B280)
  - Aged paper/cream background (#F5F5DC)
  - Limited color palette for vintage poster feel

## Technical Specs

- **Aspect Ratio:** 4:3 or 3:4 (portrait orientation)
- **Resolution:** At least 800x1000px
- **Composition:** Dramatic portrait focusing on Van Cleef's distinctive features
- **Line Work:** Bold ink lines defining facial features, especially the iconic eyes and angular face
- **Shading:** Crosshatching or watercolor washes for depth

## Additional Style Keywords

- "vintage movie poster illustration"
- "hand-drawn art"
- "painterly style"
- "artistic interpretation"
- "ink and watercolor"
- "1960s Western film poster aesthetic"
- "non-photorealistic"
- "illustrated portrait"

- Embrace imperfections and brushstroke texture

- Add paper texture overlay for aged poster feel
- Maintain strong contrast between light and shadow areas
- Apply sepia filter overlay if needed
- Consider adding subtle vignette effect around edges
